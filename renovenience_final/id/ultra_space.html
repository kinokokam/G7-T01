Review

Ultra Space Design

Date submitted
July 23, 2023

Of all the IDs we have met, we chose Ultra Space Design because they were able to solve our problems. When we bought this resale, we had issues with tiny spaces in the house but they were able to make it spacious and nice to live in. They also gave very good ideas and suggestions to our designs and when we chose our tiles, laminates, etc. I have to applaud them for their reliability and display of good responsibility. Both of them are very reliable and diligent in the sense that they will take the extra mile to go to our neighbors house and check the piping and lights so as to make sure that our house is free from water leakage and other issues. When we had problems, they will make the effort to come down and make sure everything is ok. I really appreciate their sincere effort. When we were choosing tiles, I was going back and forth with my decisions but they were patient enough to go through the process with me. The only thing that we felt could improve on is taking the height of the ceiling into consideration because when we installed the ceiling fans on the false ceiling, it was so low that we could touch it with our hands. So we thought they could have told us how low the fan will be. Otherwise, they are very helpful throughout the process of building our house. If you're looking for a reliable and trustworthy ID, you can ring them up! Many people have commented my house looks like a hotel and I have to give thanks to Ultra Space.

-
Jasmine Mong

Ultra Space Design

Date submitted
July 1, 2023

We were first timers to the renovation process and had spoken to 8 IDs. Eddie was the third ID we spoke to actually and we went back to him eventually because of the other 7 IDs we spoke to, we found that Eddie was not only detailed and always giving us undivided attention to listen how we wanted our place to look, he also impressed us with his sense of style and concept which indicated that he really has good design instincts, something that we really needed in an ID as first timers. Eddie was able to bring together many of our many ideas from themes such as scandy, modern resort, industrial and peranakan together and ensured that there was an appealing design flow throughout the house. He also made bold suggestions in areas of design that helped enhanced the overall aesthetics of the house. Before the renovation started, Eddie went through the 3D concept of our place with us thoroughly to ensured that the design was to our expectation. Eddie also provided us with a timeline of our renovation highlighting key events such as when carpentry would start and end. This was very useful as we used it to plan our moving in and other things like starting of gas and internet. We also observed Eddie has very good working relationships with his contractors which meant changes or issues could be resolved very quickly should they arise. More importantly, all his contractors had very good workmanship and during-and-after sales service. Overall, we felt that selecting Eddie was an excellent choice for us considering we are first timers. His design instincts and attention to detail coupled with excellent during-and-after service relieved us from a lot of stress as a first timer and we highly recommend Eddie to those looking for similar renovation experience!

-
Matt Liang
