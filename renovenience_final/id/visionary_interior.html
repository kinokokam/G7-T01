Visionary Interior

Date submitted
July 23, 2023

After some hiccups with the first ID under the same company, we manage to change to Joey as our interior designer. An efficient lady, and you can always trust her to reply your queries promptly (which is what we as house owners really appreciate). She has a great design sense and color coordination. We love the final outlook of our house. Ezra was also a great help with his experience and insights in assisting with details during electrical and carpentry discussion. Workmanship of the carpentry is decent but we were not given a choice to choose the internal laminate color, and ended up having all white which is not very practical since it will get dirty easily. The carpenters are great with acceding to our requests as there were constant last minute design changes on the carpentry which made sense on 3D designs but impractical and costly in reality. Painting job really needs a lot of improvement for the amount paid, as its troublesome to get the painters back to touch up, and they did not do a good job after the touch up as well.

-
Tan Justin

June 16, 2023

Choosing the right contractor is not an easy task for us, we were sceptical with most of the contractors we had met because we have quite a bit of hackings and rebuild of walls for our EM and most of them used free-hand drawings to present the ‘final’ layout, which I don’t feel comfortable to commit a 10% deposit to proceed to see the 3D drawing from the reno company. Until my husband found Visionary Interior from FB and brought me along for a second meet up. I am impressed that Lucas and Vincent printed a set of our house layout and explained layer by layer with highlights that which section we want to hack and rebuild, they also presented their company profile and a clear breakdown on their quotation. I am very pleased with their structured way of explaining and it gave me confident to engage with them. Now our house is 60% completing, Lucas being our main project manager is able to arrange different jobs timely, there always people working on our house almost every weekday, no time wasted. He is always accommodating and gives his thoughts and inputs, which I really appreciate. Unlike other contractors we have met earlier they would keep asking us what we want, which we are uncertain too. Also he is able to rectify problem fast and will always try his best to solve problems with his sub-cons to achieve our sudden requests, and I believe many other contractors would take the chance to charge extras to customers. We have started recommending to our friends. Glad that we have made the right choice !! 6 stars to you, Lucas !!

-
Marrilyne Ooi
