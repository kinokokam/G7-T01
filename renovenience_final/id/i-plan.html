The I-Plan Studio

An Overview

Founded in 2019, The I-Plan Studio transforms spaces with a focus on Aesthetics, Functionality, and Sustainability. Our talented designers specialize in innovative, tailored designs for both commercial and residential projects. Known for our quality, attention to detail, and timely execution, we craft custom interiors using premium materials. With in-house specialists in general works, tiling, and carpentry, we ensure your vision of the perfect home becomes a reality through dedicated and personalized service.

I recently had the pleasure of working with Kelvin as my interior designer, and I couldn't be more impressed with the results. From the initial consultation to the final touches, Kelvin's professionalism and attention to detail were exemplary. One aspect that truly stood out was Kelvin's ability to translate my ideas into reality. His design drawings were incredibly accurate, to the point where the final renovation looked identical to the initial renderings. This level of precision not only gave me confidence in the outcome but also made the entire process seamless and stress-free. Kelvin's expertise in interior design is evident in every aspect of his work. He not only has a keen eye for aesthetics but also a deep understanding of functionality and practicality. He was able to maximize the space in my home while creating a cohesive and visually stunning design. Throughout the project, Kelvin maintained clear communication and kept us updated on progress every step of the way. He was receptive to my feedback and made adjustments promptly, ensuring that the final result exceeded my expectations. I cannot recommend Kelvin highly enough for anyone in need of an interior designer who not only delivers exceptional results but also ensures that the vision comes to life exactly as envisioned. Thank you, Kelvin, for turning my house into a home that I absolutely love!

The I-Plan Studio

Date submitted
June 20, 2024

After being referred to Bryant Loo by my brother who was very impressed with his exceptional interior design work and iPlan Studio's good workmanship in his home last year, we decided to engage him as our ID and iPlan Studio for our own renovation project. Bryant consistently demonstrated exceptional responsiveness and efficiency, promptly addressing any issues or defects that arose during the process. Crucially, he delivered on his promise to complete the renovation within our tight two-month timeline, allowing us to move in just before Chinese New Year. When faced with unexpected challenges such as a two-week condo lift breakdown at the project's outset, Bryant handled the situation with understanding and adept coordination with his team. We greatly appreciated his consistent communication, including regular video and photo updates of the renovation progress. Furthermore, we were thoroughly impressed with the quality of iPlan Studio's in-house services and workmanship, including tiling, plumbing, carpentry, and 720° Virtual design which was exactly how our house turned out to be like. Also, Bryant's great recommendations for air conditioning, electrical work, curtain, and vinyl suppliers made our life easier and saved us time to search and liaise for one. Bryant's professionalism, patience, and approachability made the entire renovation experience smooth and less-stressful to first-time homeowners like myself. I wholeheartedly recommend Bryant Loo to anyone seeking a dependable and conscientious interior designer!

-
dizijoyjoy
