Featured Reviews

Dylan Heng - Scandinavian HDB at Dawson Road
June 7, 2024
My wife and I recently completed our home renovation with 89 Interior, and we are absolutely thrilled with the outcome. We had heard great things about 89 Interior, and they definitely delivered beyond our expectations. The designer we worked with, Ethan, took the time to understand our preferences and translated them into a design that perfectly suits our lifestyle. He had a keen eye for detail, helping us choose the right colors and materials for everything from the flooring to the cabinetry, creating a cohesive and stylish look throughout our home. Ethan's suggestions and expertise were invaluable, saving us both time and effort in the decision-making process. Throughout the project, Ethan kept us updated on the progress and was quick to address any concerns we had along the way. His dedication and responsiveness made the entire renovation journey smooth and stress-free. We’re so pleased with our beautifully renovated home and grateful to Ethan and the team at 89 Interior for their hard work and commitment. Thank you for helping us create a space we’re proud to call home!
