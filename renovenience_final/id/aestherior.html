Even after moving in, whenever we spotted some minor issues with the house, Jingwen very quickly got her contractors to come down to our house to rectify them. In the end, our reno process is smooth and everything worked out as plan and we are really appreciative of her help. Our guests were all praises of our new home! We are very grateful to her and would definitely recommend getting Jingwen as your ID!

Submitted
December 31, 2022

We were scouting around for reno companies and Aestherior was shortlisted due to the excellent reviews and appealing projects. While Caleb, our ID didn’t strike us as very conversant or interactive at our very first meeting, we could tell that he had a heart for what he was doing, choosing instead to sit and draw layouts on the floor plan with great detail and accuracy. In fact, it was by instinct and a sense that he would be the kind of ID that would pour his heart into our house reno, that we decided to go with him. This is our 2nd home and we had planned for it to be our retirement home. It was and still is a huge investment. Reno would be major as it was a 30-yr old HDB flat and we already had an idea of what we really liked and desired based on our own research and experience with our 1st home. We did spend quite a bit of time discussing and working out many details with Caleb. He was extremely patient and listened attentively to our concerns and thoughts. While he constantly seeked our input on details, he also proposed excellent choices in materials such as laminate colours and tile designs. Caleb was on site during the reno process very often to ensure the workers carried out the works well and ensured the details were looked into. He was responsive to queries and adaptive to even last minute changes. From his interactions with the different groups of workers, we could tell that he could liaise and communicate well with them. He was honest to inform us what could and could not be done and propose solutions around problems that arose. Caleb’s attitude was excellent throughout and he was highly hands-on to help out in even the nitty gritties- eg. removing paint spots on the floor, etc. What also impressed us was his humility to deal with our neighbour’s complains about noisy reno work, even personally going down at night to visit them to apologize and reassure them. We knew there was no such thing as a perfect reno work (although the carpentry was very well done) and some rectifications and touch up such as uneven plastering and paint work, after completion would be needed even after we moved in and Caleb continued to coordinate these matters. We have also heard of so many delays in reno projects all over as our country is still recovering from the pandemic and we had a tight timeline to move out of our old place to shift in. This we felt could not have been possible to a large extent without Caleb’s professionalism and strong work ethic. Caleb is indeed the ‘Heartbeat’ of our new home and we are absolutely thankful and grateful for him as our ID!

-
Jeslyn Lim

Getting a house is one of the biggest purchase to many of us and so every single decision is really important, especially your ID / contractor. My husband and I are not easy to please and we are very kiasu, so in total we enquired about 20 IDs before making the big decision. When I first contacted Caleb from Aestherior, I gave him my floor plan and told him what I want via whatsapp before meeting him. During the first meet up he already prepared the 2D as well as quotation so I was very impressed already. During the discussion we made some changes so Caleb sketch on the spot and adjusted the quotation accordingly (some of the IDs we met can’t really sketch, and some of them do not really know the costs well). We kinda knew that he is THE ONE after the first meet up mainly because, we felt that Caleb is very genuine and honest. Many IDs like to “sweet talk” and always force us to make decision quick, when we rejected them they even offered discount up to 10k, which really made us question their initial quotation and honesty. Caleb understood what we want well, we only needed a few iterations to get the 3D rendering right. During the reno process, we understand and accepted the fact that no contractor is perfect, there were some mistakes / quality issues, however Caleb always make sure to get the contractors to rectify for us. Even until now that the whole reno is completed and payment all cleared, whenever we need help Caleb always help us and sometimes he even helped us to rectify certain things on his own and he’s quite hands on. We are really glad that we have a reliable ID who can deliver, and most importantly with good attitude. Thanks Caleb for realising our dream house 😊.
