Date submitted
March 10, 2024

I recently had the pleasure of working with Don from S&R Interior, and I cannot express enough how impressed I am with his services. As a first-time homeowner, I had a previous disappointing experience with another interior designer, which made me hesitant. However, from the moment I connected with Don, I knew I was in good hands. Don's professionalism and expertise were evident right from our initial meeting. He not only listened attentively to our needs but also offered valuable insights and suggestions, particularly regarding our desire for additional storage spaces. His promptness in communication and deep understanding of what could be achieved within our budget and timeline was truly commendable. What truly sets Don apart is his efficiency and dedication to his craft. Despite the challenges presented by our project, he managed to complete the entire design process in less than a week, and finishing the project in less than two months, exceeding our expectations in every aspect. The attention to detail and quality of workmanship demonstrated throughout the project were exceptional. I am immensely grateful to Don and the team at S&R Interior Design for transforming our house into a beautiful and functional space that we now proudly call home. If you're seeking a talented, reliable, and professional interior designer, look no further than Don and S&R Interior Design. I wholeheartedly recommend their services to anyone in need of expert interior design solutions.

-
Mel Teng Hui Jun
