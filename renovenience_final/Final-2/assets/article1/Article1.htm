DIY vs Interior Designer in Singapore: Which Is Best for Your Home Renovation?

Written By
Stephen Chan
Date Written:
30/10/2024

When renovating your home, one of the first decisions you’ll face is whether to take the DIY route or to hire a professional Interior Designer (ID). Each option has its own set of advantages and challenges, and the right choice depends on your budget, vision, and the scope of the project. In Singapore’s fast-paced, space-constrained housing market, this decision becomes even more significant.


Let’s explore both sides to help you make an informed choice for your home renovation.


DIY Renovation in Singapore



DIY home renovations have gained popularity due to easy access to online resources, tutorials, and a growing interest in personalising spaces. For those who are hands-on and enjoy the creative process, DIY can be a fulfilling experience.

‍

Advantages of DIY Renovation

Cost Control

The primary advantage of DIY is the potential to save money. By cutting out labour costs associated with hiring an ID, homeowners can allocate their budget to materials or specific design features they want to splurge on. This is especially appealing in Singapore, where home renovations can be pricey.


Creative Freedom

DIY enthusiasts often appreciate the creative freedom that comes with doing everything themselves. You get to control every detail, from choosing paint colours to sourcing furniture. There's no need to compromise or explain your vision to someone else.


Personal Satisfaction

There’s a unique sense of satisfaction in transforming your own space. Completing a DIY renovation allows you to appreciate your hard work every time you step into your newly revamped home.


Challenges of DIY Renovations

Time-Consuming

Home renovation is no small feat, and the DIY route demands a significant investment of time. This can be particularly challenging in Singapore, where many homeowners juggle demanding jobs. DIY renovations often take longer than anticipated, and unexpected delays are common.


Skill Gaps

While tutorials and how-to videos can guide you, they may not fully prepare you for the complexities that arise during renovations. From electrical work to plumbing, there are tasks that require specialised knowledge. Mistakes can be costly to fix and might even cause damage in the long term.


Quality and Finish

Achieving a professional finish can be tricky without the right tools and experience. While you might be satisfied with a basic revamp, details like tiling, plumbing and lighting installation are often better handled by professionals for a polished look. After all, you wouldn’t want to pay extra and go through the embarrassment of having your work fixed, right?


Hiring an ID in Singapore



Hiring an ID brings a level of expertise, convenience, and professional insight that DIY projects may lack. In Singapore, where space optimisation and smart design are critical due to the small size of most HDB flats and condominiums, working with an ID can make a significant difference.


Advantages of Hiring an ID

Expertise and Experience

IDs bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to the table. From design principles to technical skills, they know how to make the most of a space. They’re trained to create cohesive designs that balance functionality with aesthetics, ensuring your home looks great and works for your lifestyle.


Access to Resources

IDs have access to a network of suppliers, contractors, and materials that aren’t easily available to the average homeowner. This means they can source high-quality products at competitive prices. In Singapore, where specific materials or furniture styles can be hard to find, having an ID's connections can be invaluable.


Project Management

Renovation projects require meticulous planning and coordination. When you hire an ID, they take care of the entire process – from design conceptualisation to managing contractors and timelines. This level of project management ensures that your renovation stays on track, saving you from the headaches of dealing with delays, supplier issues, or miscommunications.


Space Optimisation

In Singapore’s compact homes, every square metre counts. IDs specialise in maximising small spaces, making them more functional and visually appealing. Whether it’s incorporating smart storage solutions or using multifunctional furniture, an ID will ensure your home feels spacious, even with limited room.


Challenges of Hiring an ID

Cost

One of the most significant drawbacks of hiring an ID is the cost. While they can help you avoid costly mistakes and may have access to trade discounts, the overall cost of hiring a professional is generally higher than a DIY renovation. However, if you're looking for a high-quality finish and want to avoid the stress of managing the project yourself, the investment can be worth it.


Less Creative Control

While IDs work closely with clients to bring their vision to life, you may need to compromise on certain design elements. Some homeowners find it challenging to let go of creative control, preferring to make every design decision themselves. That said, a good ID will always prioritise your preferences while incorporating their professional insight. Remember, they want what’s best for your home too!


Potential Miscommunication

Any renovation project requires clear communication, and this is no different when working with an ID. If there’s a lack of communication, the final design may not turn out exactly as you envisioned. However, this risk can be mitigated by thoroughly vetting your ID and ensuring you maintain regular discussions throughout the project.


DIY vs Hiring an ID: A Direct Comparison


DIY vs ID: The Right Choice for Your Home Renovation



Choosing between DIY and hiring an ID ultimately depends on several factors: your budget, the complexity of the renovation, your personal skills, and the amount of time you can commit. If you’re looking for a fun, hands-on project and have the time to spare, DIY can be a great way to personalise your space. On the other hand, if you prefer a hassle-free process with professional results, hiring an ID may be the better option.
