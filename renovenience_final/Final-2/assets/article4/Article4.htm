5 Smart Home Upgrades You Need Right Now From Futurahaus

Written By
Karen Chua
Date Written:
4/10/2024

Imagine this: It is finally the December holidays, and you’ve just landed in Bali with your family for vacation. However, you suddenly recall that your living room lights are still shining bright as you left home in a hurry this morning to catch the flight. Thankfully, that is now no longer a problem, as you have upgraded your home to have smart features from Futurahaus  last month! Your wife whips out the app on her phone, and with one tap the lights are turned off and you can continue vacationing without worries. That is the power of upgrading to a smart home.

‍

You’re probably thinking that owning smart homes is a privilege for the rich. However, we’re here to introduce you to this industry-revolutionising brand—Futurahaus! Founded in 2021, Futurahaus offers professional installation of smart home solutions at affordable prices, while providing excellent customer service and post-purchase support.

‍

Despite their competitive pricing, Futurahaus ensures high quality by partnering with leading global smart-home brands such as Control4 and Lutron—trusted by over half a million customers! 

‍

It is common in the industry where smart-home retailers sell products that create smart houses instead of smart homes—offering you products with an array of features that appear impressive, but have no real use in daily life. On the contrary, Futurahaus offers smart products that intentionally target pain points of daily life, offering utmost convenience and a peace of mind.

‍

Here are five smart homes upgrades that you absolutely need in this day and age:

‍

1. Mood Maestro: Conduct Your Lights With A Single Tap To Create The Perfect Ambience For Any Occasion

Smart lighting goes beyond switching your lights on and off with your mobile phone; it transforms lighting fixtures into smart tools that can elevate the ambience and functionality of your living environment.

‍

Have you ever watched a theatre performance where different light settings are used to convey a specific mood and atmosphere for different scenes? Futurahaus has something similar as well, allowing you to customise scenes based on your lifestyle.

‍

‍

You’ve finally arrived at your doorstep after a long day at work: instead of manually switching on every light you need and twisting the dimming knobs multiple times to achieve the best post-work ambience, you can simply set your smart lighting to the “Welcome” scene.

‍

‍

‍

Light floods the entire living, dining and kitchen area with just a single tap, warmly welcoming you home.

‍

‍

Scenes refer to combinations of lighting options for different occasions, customised based on your daily needs. During movie nights, you may select a “Movie” scene. A "Movie" scene doesn't just dim the lights—it can also draw the curtains, turn on the TV, and switch directly to Netflix, all in one go. It’s more than just adjusting brightness; it’s about creating the perfect ambiance for any occasion, much like your own private cinema experience at home, with soft, subtle lighting enhancing your movie nights.

‍

Rushing for work? Press the “Goodbye” button, and all of your lighting fixtures will switch off automatically. No more scrambling from room-to-room, switching off each light manually.

‍

Besides customising scenes, there are many thoughtful features Futurahaus’ Control4 application provides.

‍

‍

Smart lighting saves space and reduces visual clutter as dimming knobs are no longer needed. You may adjust the brightness of each smart lighting from your smartphone conveniently, using a slider.

‍

‍

‍

Concerned that the aesthetic of light switches may not fit the theme of your dream home? These smart switches come in all sorts of colours, configurations and materials, so you do not have to worry about it looking out of place!

‍

2. Wire-free Elegance: Carry The World’s First Ever Smart Metal Kinetic Switch In Your Pocket

Regular switches operate by being a component in the electrical circuit, which requires lots of hidden wiring. This limits the number of locations the switches can be mounted at, which reduces flexibility of design.

‍

On the other hand, kinetic switches such as Janus switches from Futura by Futurahaus can be mounted at any location, wire-free.

‍

‍

Kinetic switches do not require electrical wiring as these switches are powered by kinetic energy. The motion of clicking the switch transmits a signal to your lamp, switching it on or off, which makes it portable! You can choose to carry it around or mount it to any surface you prefer.

‍

‍

The Janus switches, developed by Futura in collaboration with Sol Luminaire, stands out for a few other reasons as well. Janus switches are IP67 rated, which means that they are water and dust resistant. This makes it durable and easy to maintain, as the stylistic surface will not tarnish easily in face of everyday wear and tear.

‍

‍

Additionally, the basic series is made from plastic and comes in all sorts of colours, ranging from space grey to white. The premium series, made from metal, offers finishings such as brushed aluminium and brushed gold, acting as statement pieces in homes of all styles.

‍

‍

These kinetic switches start at a fair price point of $69!

‍

3. Total Home Security: Peace Of Mind Wherever You Are

Have a peace of mind wherever you are, through the power of Futurahaus’ security system. Full CCTV coverage in high definition allows you to visually access every corner of your home from anywhere around the world, directly from your phone.

‍

‍

Worried about the security of your own home after seeing news of bizarre occurrences outside other people’s houses? Fret not, as the security system Futurahaus offers sends you notifications immediately when it senses a threat. This ensures that you are always up-to-date with the state of your home.

‍

Have a visitor but you are still on your way back home? Your guest can ring the doorbell and communicate with you via your remote device. After confirming the identity of your guest, you can then unlock the door for your guest remotely from a single tap on your device. This is particularly useful in everyday scenarios such as requiring a friend or family member to tend to your houseplants while you are on vacation!

‍

‍

4. Start Your Day Right: Automated Curtain Tracks For Effortless Morning Sunshine

Besides the automation of smart lighting, Futurahaus also provides automated curtain tracks! In the mornings where you’d be heavy-eyed and groggy, going through the motion of opening your curtains may be a dreadful task. With the automated curtain tracks, you can set a time for the curtains to open, rather than manually opening them only after you have mustered the strength to do so. The sunlight exposure suppresses melatonin and increases cortisol, which is essential to helping you feel alert in the morning! This smart-curtain feature will help you bathe in the natural sunlight—a fabulous way to start your morning.

‍

‍

Similar to smart lighting, you may also control the movement of your curtains from your preferred choice of electronic device (tablet or mobile phone). On days where you happen to forget to close your curtains, you can simply close them remotely to protect the privacy of your abode.

‍

‍

5. Home Theatre: Your Personal Luxurious Movie Cinema

When it comes to movie nights, the key aspects that differentiate a home theatre from a standard set-up are its audio and visual capabilities. With Futurahaus’ home theatre system, you can enjoy your weekly movies in an in-house smart cinema.

‍

‍

Control the settings of each component of your luxurious home theatre remotely on your mobile phone, instead of having to walk everywhere to set-up your cinematic experience. You can also play movies from your desired streaming source directly from your mobile device.

‍

‍

An immersive movie night would not be possible without excellent audio equipment. Futurahaus supports stereo sound, surround sound, and even Dolby Atmos (yes, the exact one they use in movie theatres!). Rewatch your favourite movies with the same cinematic experience in the comfort and convenience of your own home! 

‍
