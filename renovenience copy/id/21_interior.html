We had the pleasure of working with Cheryl as our interior designer for our recent renovation project, and we couldn't be happier with the results. From the start, she impressed us with her ability to understand and incorporate our ideas seamlessly. She provided valuable tips and suggestions, ensuring that our plans were perfected and met our expectations. As parents, it was essential for us to have a child-friendly design, and Cheryl completely understood our needs. She incorporated thoughtful details like rounded corners into our carpentry, creating a safe and functional space for our family. Cheryl's meticulous planning and attention to detail truly set her apart. She carefully scheduled the project, keeping us informed about the days when she needed our presence and even coordinated with us for the delivery of appliances and fixtures. Her proactive communication made the process smooth and hassle-free, especially considering our busy schedules. Throughout the project, Cheryl demonstrated a commitment to quality. She provided regular updates and photos, allowing us to monitor the progress without needing to be physically present. Not only did Cheryl excel in delivering a beautiful and well-executed design, she also amazed us with the project's timeline. Despite the tight schedule, she managed to complete the tiling, carpentry, and installation within two months, allowing us to move in as soon as possible. We are grateful to Cheryl for expediting the process and delivering on time. In conclusion, working with Cheryl was an outstanding experience, and se wholeheartedly recommend her as an interior designer. Her creativity, professionalism, and dedication to customer satisfaction deserve a perfect rating.
