Welcome

With 4 years of global experience in residential, hospitality, industrial design, and creative direction, Renovenience focuses on re-sensualizing the built environment. Founded in 2020 in Singapore, Renovenience is a multidisciplinary design practice dedicated to enhancing well-being through minimalist yet haptic designs that engage both mind and body.

Our work—spanning architecture, interiors, and products—seeks to uncover the essence of design, creating spaces that fulfill the human desire for belonging, autonomy, and identity. We strive for simplicity, finding beauty in function and ensuring each creation feels as good as it looks.

Featured Projects

Timber Cabin Redesign

Renovenience has transformed a traditional timber cabin, nestled among Sweden's pine forests, into a minimalist family retreat.

Taking a "back-to-basics" approach, the Copenhagen-based studio remodeled the two-floor cabin perched on a ridge, emphasizing simplicity and authenticity.

Seaside Sanctuary Home

Inspired by Laguna Beach’s bohemian spirit, this Seaside Sanctuary design by Renovenience blends calming neutral tones with original terracotta flooring and a captivating plaster mural of a "sun-worshipping goddess."

Drawing from Majorcan influences, the redesign creates an organic, serene space that embodies the essence of a tranquil seaside retreat.

Minimalist Cabin Retreat

Renovenience transforms a traditional cabin into a serene, minimalist retreat. The project embodies an exercise in restraint, resulting in a simple, authentic, and welcoming space that balances aesthetics with comfort.

Featuring greige sofas, a sheepskin lounge chair, and a wood burner set against dolomite-plastered walls, the design emphasizes simplicity, comfort, and authenticity.

FAQ

+

How much will it cost?

To help you estimate the cost of your project, visit our interior designs page. Choose a design based on square meter size and aesthetic preference, and explore ballpark figures provided for each featured project by our partnered ID firms.

+

How long it takes to design?

The timeline for any assignment is typically set by the ID. If you have a specific timeframe in mind, we will do our best to negotiate with the ID on your behalf.

+

What services do you offer, and what is included in your packages?

We offer comprehensive interior design services, including space planning, project management, and post-completion follow-ups. Our packages are tailored to meet various needs, from simple consultations to full-service design and project management.

+

Can I see examples of your completed projects?

Absolutely. We feature a gallery of our best projects from top boutique IDs in Singapore, showcasing various styles and sizes. You can explore these to get a sense of our work and quality standards.

+

Are there any additional fees or hidden costs?

We believe in transparent pricing. All fees will be discussed upfront, and any additional costs will be communicated in advance to avoid surprises.
